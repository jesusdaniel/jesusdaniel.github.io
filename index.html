I am a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Center for Imaging Science at Johns Hopkins University, working with Professors Carey E. Priebe and Joshua T. Vogelstein. Previously, I completed my PhD in the Department of Statistics at the University of Michigan, advised by Professor Liza Levina.

My research interests include statistical network analysis, machine learning and high dimensional data analysis, and applications to neuroimaging.

I received a B.Sc. in Applied Mathematics and a B.Sc. in Computer Engineering at Mexico Autonomous Institute of Technology (ITAM).
